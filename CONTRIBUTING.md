# Contributing to GRC Practice Lab

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

GRC Practice Lab is a project-based GRC simulation platform designed to help students and early-career professionals practice real-world governance, risk, and compliance workflows.

We welcome thoughtful contributions that improve:

- Usability
- Accuracy of GRC workflows
- Educational clarity
- UI/UX
- Performance
- Documentation
