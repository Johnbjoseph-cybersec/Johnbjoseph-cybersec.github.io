Terms of Use

The GRC Practice Lab is a hands-on environment I use in my content to demonstrate how real governance, risk, and compliance work is done. It includes a fictional company, realistic risks, and full documentation.

Educational Use Only This lab is built on fictional companies and isolated virtual machines. It is designed purely for education and professional training. No real-world systems, networks, or organizations are accessed or affected. All activities follow legal and ethical guidelines for cybersecurity learning.

  • Fictional SaaS company with defined scope
  • Risk register, impact/likelihood scoring, owners
  • Annex A control mapping and SoA examples
  • Vendor risk assessment and due diligence examples
  • AI governance and ISO 42001 scenarios

  • Risk Register (spreadsheet + views)
  • Statement of Applicability (SoA) sample
  • Annex A controls mapping to a real environment
  • Vendor risk questionnaire and ratings
  • Policy and standard examples (Information Security, Access Control, etc.)
